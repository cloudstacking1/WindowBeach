FAQ's

Window film is a thin, multi-layered polyester material applied to the interior of windows. It offers numerous benefits, including increased privacy, reduced glare, UV protection, and enhanced energy efficiency by minimizing heat transfer. This helps in lowering energy bills and protecting your interior furnishings from fading.

The duration of the installation depends on the size and number of windows being tinted. Typically, a complete home installation can take several hours to a full day. Our professional installers at Window Tinting Pros Palm Beach Gardens ensure a precise and efficient application process.

After installation, it's advisable to wait a week before cleaning the tinted windows. Use a mild, non-ammonia-based window cleaner and a soft cloth or rubber squeegee to clean the surface. Avoid using abrasive materials or sharp tools that could scratch or damage the film.
